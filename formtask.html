Kurnool

The original name of Kurnool is found in historical records as
Kandanavﾅ考u or Kandanﾅ考u. It used to be a crossing on the Tungabhadra River,
where the bullock cart caravans are believed to have greased their wheels ("kandana" being a reference to grease).
The city is often referred to as "The Gateway of Rayalaseema".
